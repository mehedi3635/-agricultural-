Crop Details

Rise

Varieties:
Indica, Japonica, and Aromatic.

Cultivation Methods:
  • Rice is grown in flooded fields or paddies, which helps to control
  • It requires warm temperatures (20–35°C)
Soil Requirements:
  • Well-drained, loamy soil with a pH of 5.5 to 7.
Wheat

Varieties:
Hard Red Winter, Soft Red Winter, Hard Red Spring, and Durum.

Cultivation Methods:
  • Grown in temperate climates with cool winters and warm summers.
Soil Requirements:
  • Prefers deep, fertile, and well-drained soils with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5.
Apples

Varieties:
Fuji, Gala, Granny Smith, Red Delicious.

Cultivation Methods
  • equires a temperate climate with cold winters for dormancy.
  • Grown on dwarf or semi-dwarf trees for ease of harvesting.
Soil Requirements
  • Well-drained, loamy soil with a slightly acidic pH (6.0 to 6.5).
Bananas

Varieties
Cavendish, Lady Finger, Red Banana.

Cultivation Methods:
  • Grown in tropical and subtropical regions with temperatures between 14–30°C.
  • Needs a lot of water and sunlight.
